IEEE LAUNCHPAD KERALA 2024


Introduction

Launchpad Kerala 2024, a flagship event jointly organized by IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, is a prestigious job fair that connects top talent from engineering institutions with cutting-edge companies in the technical and engineering sectors. This strategic collaboration aims to drive innovation and progress in Kerala's employment ecosystem by facilitating meaningful connections, idea sharing, and expertise exchange between promising individuals and forward-thinking organizations.


Launchpad Kerala 2024 is a premier platform designed to unleash the potential of Kerala's tech talent! This event offers a unique opportunity for tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills, connect with industry leaders, and kickstart their dream careers. Whether you're a budding coder, an innovative entrepreneur, or a seasoned professional, Launchpad Kerala welcomes you to be a part of this transformative journey. Seize this chance to network, learn, and grow with the best in the industry. Take the first step towards catapulting your career to new heights. Join Launchpad Kerala 2024 and unlock your true potential


Important Update: Online Aptitude Test and Mock Test Details

Online Aptitude Test Details:

The online aptitude test is scheduled for July 4th, and participation is mandatory for all candidates aiming to proceed to the interview round. Your performance in this test, along with your technical challenge results, will determine your position in the final rank list. Interviews will be conducted in Trivandrum and Kochi based on this ranking.


Mock Test Preparation:

To help you prepare effectively, we will be conducting a mock test on July 3rd. Further details regarding this will be shared in a separate email soon.


Eligibility Criteria:

lease note that only those who have completed at least one task in the Technical Challenge by July 1st will be eligible to participate in the aptitude test. If you haven't started the technical challenge yet, we encourage you to begin at your earliest convenience. You can find detailed instructions on how to start your technical challenges here.


Act Now:

Remember, the deadline to start your technical challenge is July 1st. This is a crucial step towards launching your career with Launchpad Kerala. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your skills and potential.


Event Roadmap

Step 1: Online Registration - You have successfully completed the registration process, securing your place at Launchpad Kerala 2024.


Step 2: Confirmation - Candidates will receive an email from the Launchpad Kerala 2024 team within 48 hours of registration. All updates will be posted on the updates page of the Launchpad Kerala 2024 website.


Step 3: Join the Community - Join the Gtech Mulearn Discord Server and complete the Launchpad Challenges.


Step 4: Technical Challenge - Find levels and tasks related to the IEEE Launchpad category in the #ieee-launchpad-announcement channel on Discord. Submit tasks on time to earn Karma points.


Step 5: Online Aptitude Test - Participate in the online assessment to showcase your competencies to potential employers.


Step 6: Await the Finalized Results - Your performance in the technical challenge and online test will be evaluated. Stay tuned for the results.


Step 7: Final Interview - The final stage of the selection process where you can showcase your skills and secure your dream job.
